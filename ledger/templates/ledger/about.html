About I&F Bank

Founded in 1998, I&F Bank began as a small community savings institution in Kigali with a simple promise: to put people before paperwork. Over the decades, I&F Bank has grown into a trusted financial partner for individuals and businesses across Rwanda, combining modern digital banking with the personal, relationship-driven service the bank was built on.

Our Motto

"Your Trust, Our Foundation."

Our Values

  • Integrity — We safeguard every shilling and every secret entrusted to us.
  • Accessibility — Banking should reach every community, not just city centers.
  • Innovation — We invest continuously in technology that makes banking simpler.
  • Respect — Every customer is treated with dignity, regardless of account size.
  • Accountability — We answer for our actions, to our customers and regulators alike.

Leadership

I&F Bank is led by Fabrice Iradukunda, Chief Executive Officer, whose vision has guided the bank's transformation into a modern, digitally-driven financial institution while preserving its founding commitment to customer trust and community impact.
